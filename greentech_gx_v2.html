Our Story

Driving Sustainable Change
Across South Asia

GreenTech GX International was founded on a straightforward conviction: that clean, technologically advanced energy and mobility should not be exclusive to wealthy nations or wealthy individuals. We build for the roads, rivers, and wind patterns of South Asia — not repurposed imports.

From Kathmandu, we design electric vehicles, hydropower turbines, and wind energy systems that are purpose-built for this region's unique geography, climate, and infrastructure realities.

2020
Founded in Kathmandu

GreenTech GX established with a vision to bring clean energy and electric mobility to South Asia.

2021
First Scooter Launched

The GX Velocity Gen 1 went on sale, selling 500 units in its first month across Nepal.

2022
Hydropower Division Launched

First run-of-river turbine installations completed in Kaski and Myagdi districts, powering over 200 households.

2023
Expanded to India

Opened 20 service centres across Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore. Wind energy division launched.

2025
5,000 Vehicles on Road

Launched the GX Aura Sedan and GX Terra SUV. Now serving 5,000+ customers across 30 cities.
